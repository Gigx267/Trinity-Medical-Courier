About Us

Industry Insight Meets Operational Excellence

At Trinity Medical Courier, we specialize in the secure, compliant, and time-sensitive transport of medical specimens, pharmaceuticals, and critical healthcare materials.

Founded by a former manufacturing associate at Bristol Myers Squibb—one of the top global biopharmaceutical companies—our company is built on firsthand industry insight and operational excellence.

Having worked inside one of the most respected names in pharma, we understand the strict demands of GMP environments and the importance of every delivery in the chain of patient care.

Our Mission

Bridging Healthcare Logistics

To serve as the reliable bridge between the patient and the industry—delivering with care, precision, and urgency.

We proudly support labs, clinics, pharmacies, and manufacturers across Massachusetts and beyond, offering logistics solutions rooted in accountability, speed, and industry-grade standards.

From Pharma. For Patients.
