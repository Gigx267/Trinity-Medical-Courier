About Us

Industry Insight Meets Operational Excellence

At Trinity Medical Courier, we specialize in the secure, compliant, and time-sensitive transport of medical specimens, pharmaceuticals, and critical healthcare materials.

Founded by a former manufacturing associate at Bristol Myers Squibb—one of the top global biopharmaceutical companies—our company is built on firsthand industry insight and operational excellence.
